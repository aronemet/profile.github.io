Account Deletion

Last updated: March 2026

If you would like to delete your data associated with Sudoku - Classic Sudoku Puzzle, please submit this request.

Important:

This game uses Google Play Games Services and Firebase services for features such as authentication, analytics, and gameplay functionality.

Data that may be collected:

  • User ID (via Google Play Games or authentication)
  • Gameplay data such as scores and achievements
  • Device and usage data (via analytics and ads)

What we can delete:

  • Any data stored by the developer in Firebase (such as gameplay data, if applicable)
  • Locally stored data on your device

Third-party services:

Some data is processed and stored by third-party services such as Google Play Games and Google services. These services manage your account data independently.

For full account or Google data deletion:

Please visit: https://myaccount.google.com/delete-services-or-account

Data retention:

  • Data deletion requests are processed within 7 days.
  • Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance before permanent deletion.

How to delete locally stored data:

To delete data stored on your device, you can:

  • Uninstall the app from your device
  • Clear the app's data and cache through your device settings
  • Use your device's app management tools to remove all associated data
